The N-number checker will go through this spreadsheet and correct any invalid N-numbers.

For example:

  • 12345678 ---> N12345678
  • n12345678 ---> N12345678
  • N-12345678 ---> N12345678
  • N-12345678 ---> N12345678

Additionally, any spaces around valid N-numbers will be removed.

N-numbers that are one digit too long or too short will be highlighted (in violet, of course).
